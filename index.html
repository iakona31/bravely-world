Ancient Lore

A World Forged in Valor

✦

In an age before memory, the First Fires were lit across the realm — not by gods, but by those who dared to face the darkness with open hands. This is Bravely World: a living, breathing universe of myth, conflict, and extraordinary courage.

Across seven kingdoms and countless wilds, the threads of fate weave together warriors, wanderers, scholars, and outcasts — each carrying a piece of a prophecy older than the stars above.

Bravely World is more than a story. It is a place you can inhabit — through our novels, games, and artifacts — a world that grows richer with every tale told.
